Emergency Resource Sharing App Wireframe
An App prototype for resource sharing in an emergency situation. This app would be based around a Map and work similar to AirBnB or other resource sharing apps. Users would be able to post what resources they have available, what they need, see where these resources are available, what areas are hazardous, the best routes to the things they need and be able to chat with other users. The idea behind this app is to decentralize resource centers, such as grocery stores, in times of emergency.
