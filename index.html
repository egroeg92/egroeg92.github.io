    About
    George Macrae is a Designer and Developer who lives in Vancouver, BC. He is currently a 4th year Communication Design Student at Emily Carr University, and has a BSc major Computer Science minor Physics from McGill University in Quebec.

    George’s focus is to combine his expertise in Development with his passion for Design to create practical and meaningful products. He looks forward to a career in User Focused Design and Development.
